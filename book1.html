A Song in Space – Kesarbai Kerkar

Little Kesar loved to sing. She never imagined that when she grew up her voice would travel to distant galaxies.

It's dreamy, beautiful and inspiring.

The story traces Kesarbai Kerkar's musical journey through space. She remains the only Indian whose voice has travelled beyond Earth—immortalized on NASA's 1977 Golden Record. Creating this non‑fiction book required deep research and fact‑checking, down to details like Carl Sagan's trademark turtlenecks. I handled the character design, cover art, and all interior illustrations.
