Aurko's Museum Adventure

Aurko loves paintings. What happens when he is taken to the museum?

Emotional, fantastical, playful.

This book is part of a series on Indian fine artists. It's about Abanindranath Tagore's work and is filled with imagination, and surprise. I illustrated this story with dynamic compositions, vibrant colors, and a sense of childlike wonder to mirror Aurko's energetic journey through the exhibits.
