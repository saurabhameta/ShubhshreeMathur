Chik-chik girl

Mona loves to complain. Sometimes about the tasty khichdi her Amma cooks and sometimes about Dada's long legs. Meet the chik-chik girl and her family.

Relatable, funny, homely!!

This story is not just about Mona, but also an insight into her home and her family. The cultural nuance of being a middle-class family from North India was the focal point. It was also an opportunity for me to subtly show a small town family with progressive outlook where the father is involved in the things like folding clothes and making food.

Written By - Komilla Raote
