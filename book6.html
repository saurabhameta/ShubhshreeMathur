Dadi ki Choodiyaan

Munnu's accidently breaks his Dadi's beautiful bangles. What will happen now? What will Dadi say?

It is heartfelt, emotional and full of love.

This book is a funny yet heartfelt delight for early readers. The story is set in old city Jodhpur and hence the backdrop of indigo walls feels enchanting. It is published in Hindi and translated into multiple regional languages from Rajasthan.
