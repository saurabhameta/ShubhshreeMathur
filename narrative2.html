Besharmi (Shamelessness)

My father was away most of the time for work untill COVID happened and he had to return. It led to my parents spending some quality time with each other, but our neighbours didn't look at it that way. Since they had never seen my father, they thought my mother was living with a new man.

A narrative subtly capturing the invasive and judgemental nature of people around us. Commentary on human behaviour.
