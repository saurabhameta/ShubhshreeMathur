Hadda

An endearing story of how my father prepared unconventional baby food for me and broke traditional norms at various levels. During a time when men did not go in the kitchen or help around with household work, my father loved to cook. He went to study the same, going against his family. When he had me, he experimented with all sorts of homemade baby food, which was frequently frowned upon. This narrative was a way for me to document his recipes and also show my love for him.

(A Part of" Memories on Plate" Anthology)
